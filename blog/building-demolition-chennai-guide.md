## Legal Requirements in Chennai

### Permissions Required

**1. Chennai Corporation Approval**
- Application for demolition permission
- Building plan and structural drawings
- Structural stability certificate
- NOC from neighbors (if applicable)

**Processing time:** 7-15 days

**2. Environmental Clearance**
Required for buildings with:
- Asbestos materials
- Hazardous waste
- Large debris volume (>500 tons)

**3. Utility Disconnection Certificates**
- Electricity disconnection (TANGEDCO)
- Water connection closure (Chennai Metro Water)
- Gas connection closure (if applicable)

**4. Traffic Police NOC**
Required if demolition affects:
- Main roads
- Public transport routes
- Heavy traffic areas

## Demolition Process Step-by-Step

### Step 1: Site Inspection & Assessment (Day 1)
- Structural evaluation
- Hazard identification
- Access assessment
- Equipment planning
- Cost estimation

### Step 2: Permission & Documentation (Week 1)
- Submit applications to Chennai Corporation
- Obtain utility disconnections
- Secure necessary NOCs
- Arrange insurance coverage

### Step 3: Site Preparation (Day 1-2)
- Install safety barriers
- Put up warning signage
- Disconnect all utilities
- Remove valuable materials
- Set up debris management system

### Step 4: Controlled Demolition (Days 3-10)
**For Multi-Story Buildings:**
- Top-down approach (floor by floor)
- Controlled structural weakening
- Systematic dismantling
- Continuous safety monitoring

**Equipment Used:**
- Hydraulic excavators (20-50 ton)
- Concrete breakers
- Hydraulic hammers
- Demolition balls (for large structures)
- Cutting equipment

### Step 5: Debris Removal & Recycling (Days 5-12)
- Segregate materials (metal, concrete, wood)
- Recover valuable scrap (steel, copper)
- Transport debris to authorized sites
- Recycle reusable materials

### Step 6: Site Clearing & Leveling (Final 2-3 days)
- Remove all debris
- Level the ground
- Clean surrounding areas
- Final inspection
- Handover clean site

**Total Timeline:** 1-4 weeks depending on building size
